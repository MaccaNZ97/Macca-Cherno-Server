===================================== Pvz_TheDarkHorde_TerrainLimits.xml =====================================

This file set the total map area. It is required for random or hunting movements types.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

"ZONE_TO_USE"
	[Text]
	Here you set the zone name (from the other sections of this file) you want to use.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

"Zone_Name"
	[Text]
	
"TopLeft_Corner_Coordinates" and "LowRight_Corner_Coordinates"
	[Integer/Integer]
	Limits of the zone (Imagine you define a rectangle with its two opposite corners).
	
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
